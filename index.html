Order of Rituals

01
Afternoonअतिथि स्वागतGuest Arrival
▾
Guests welcomed with garlands, tilaka blessings and traditional Nepali sweets. The mandap is adorned and the sacred fire prepared.अतिथिहरूलाई माला र तिलकले स्वागत।
02
5:00 PMजन्ती / बरातJanti Procession
▾
The groom's procession arrives with music and celebration — family and friends accompany Milan to the venue.संगीत र उत्सवसहित जन्ती आउँछ।
03
5:45 PMमाला ओढाईVarmala Exchange
▾
Milan and Deepa exchange marigold garlands — a symbol of acceptance and the beginning of their sacred bond.मिलन र दीपाले सयपत्री माला साटासाट गर्छन्।
04
6:30 PMविवाह संस्कारSacred Vows
▾
Vedic mantras recited before the sacred fire as Milan and Deepa take their vows, witnessed by all.पण्डितले पवित्र अग्निको अगाडि वैदिक मन्त्र पाठ।
05
7:15 PMसप्तपदीSeven Sacred Steps
▾
Seven steps together around the holy fire — nourishment, strength, prosperity, happiness, progeny, health, eternal friendship.सात पाइला — अन्न, बल, सम्पत्ति, सुख, सन्तान, स्वास्थ्य, मित्रता।
06
8:00 PMभोजGrand Nepali Feast
▾
Sel roti, dal bhat, tarkari, and traditional sweets — two families celebrate becoming one.सेल रोटी, दाल भात, तरकारी र मिठाइ।
07
9:30 PMसंगीत र नृत्यSangeet & Dancing
▾
Lok dohori, folk songs, and dancing — a night of pure joy remembered for generations.लोक दोहोरी र नृत्यले दुई परिवार एक हुन्छन्।
