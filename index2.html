Dear Beloved
Special Guest

You Are Our Story

A wedding without its witnesses is merely a ceremony — with you, it becomes a memory that breathes forever. Every blessing you carry through our door, every smile you gift us on this day, becomes the very ink with which our love story is written.

विवाह भनेको दुई मान्छेको मात्र मिलन होइन — यो त दुई संसारको, दुई कथाको मिलन हो। तपाईं त्यही मायाको अटुट धागो हुनुहुन्छ।

🕯️साक्षी
You Are Our Witness

Hindu tradition holds that a marriage exists only in the eyes of those who witness it. You make our vows real, eternal, and blessed.

🏔️आशीर्वादको पहाड
A Mountain of Blessings

Like the Himalayas that stand eternal — your blessing is the mountain beneath our new beginning.

🌺हाम्रो खुशीको रंग
A Colour in Our Joy

Your presence, your smile — these are the colours woven into our love story. Without you, a colour is missing.

🪔परिवारको विस्तार
Family Beyond Blood

You were chosen — not by birth, but by love. That makes you more than a guest. You are family at our table.

Two Holy Mantras

सप्तपदी — प्रथम पाइला · First Sacred Step
ॐ इषे एकपदी भव।
सा मामनुव्रता भव।
पुत्रान् विन्दावहै बहूँस्ते
सन्तु जरदष्टयः।
"With this first sacred step we walk together for nourishment and sustenance — may we always care for one another in body, mind and soul, and may our bond grow stronger with every step we take in this life and beyond."
पशुपतिनाथको आशीर्वाद · Lord Shiva's Eternal Blessing
ॐ नमः शिवाय।
हर हर महादेव।
जय बाबा पशुपतिनाथ।
सुखी भव, दीर्घायु भव।
"We bow before Lord Shiva — the eternal witness of all sacred unions in Nepal. May Pashupatinath bless Milan and Deepa with everlasting love, health, peace and prosperity. May this home always be a home of light."

The Sacred Programme

01
Afternoon · दिउँसो अतिथि स्वागत Guest Arrival & Welcome
▾
Guests are welcomed with garlands, tilaka blessings, and traditional sweets. The mandap is adorned and the sacred fire is prepared.अतिथिहरूलाई माला र तिलकले स्वागत गरिन्छ। मण्डप सजाइन्छ र पवित्र अग्नि तयार गरिन्छ।
02
5:00 PM जन्ती / बरात प्रस्थान Janti Procession
▾
The groom's procession arrives with music and celebration — family and friends accompany Milan to the wedding venue in joyful ceremony.बरको जन्ती संगीत र उत्सवसहित आउँछ। परिवार र साथीभाइले मिलनलाई विवाह स्थलसम्म साथ दिन्छन्।
03
5:45 PM माला ओढाई Varmala — Garland Exchange
▾
Milan and Deepa exchange marigold garlands — a beautiful symbol of acceptance and the beginning of their sacred bond before all witnesses.मिलन र दीपाले सयपत्री माला साटासाट गर्छन् — यो उनीहरूको पवित्र बन्धनको सुन्दर प्रतीक हो।
04
6:30 PM विवाह संस्कार Vivah Sanskar — Sacred Vows
▾
The pandit recites Vedic mantras before the sacred fire as Milan and Deepa take their vows — witnessed by their families and the gods above.पण्डितले पवित्र अग्निको अगाडि वैदिक मन्त्र पाठ गर्छन् र मिलन-दीपाले प्रतिज्ञा लिन्छन्।
05
7:15 PM सप्तपदी Saptapadi — Seven Sacred Steps
▾
Seven steps taken together around the holy fire — each step a promise for nourishment, strength, prosperity, happiness, progeny, health, and everlasting friendship.सात पाइला — अन्न, बल, सम्पत्ति, सुख, सन्तान, स्वास्थ्य र मित्रताको वाचा।
06
8:00 PM भोज तथा उत्सव Bhoj — Feast & Celebration
▾
A grand Nepali feast is shared by all — rice, dal, tarkari, and sweets. Music fills the air as the two families celebrate becoming one.भव्य नेपाली भोज — भात, दाल, तरकारी र मिठाइ। संगीत र नृत्यले रात भर उत्सव मनाइन्छ।
07
9:30 PM संगीत र नृत्य Sangeet & Dancing
▾
The night comes alive — lok dohori, folk songs, and dancing as two families become one. A night of joy that will be remembered for generations.रात जीवित हुन्छ — लोक दोहोरी, लोक गीत र नृत्यले दुई परिवार एक हुन्छन्। यो रात पुस्तौंसम्म याद गरिनेछ।
ॐ

ॐ द्यौः शान्तिरन्तरिक्षं शान्तिः।
पृथिवी शान्तिरापः शान्तिः।
ओषधयः शान्तिर्वनस्पतयः शान्तिः।
विश्वे देवाः शान्तिर्ब्रह्म शान्तिः।

"दुई हृदय, एक आत्मा —
Two hearts, one eternal soul."
